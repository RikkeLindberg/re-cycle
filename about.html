About re-cycle

We are a traveling agency located in Svolvær. Our goal is to make traveling in Lofoten eco-friendly. By restoring old bikes and making them good as new, they can be used again and give people an experience of nature and culture they wouldn't get by driving or flying. We want everyone to be able to enjoy Lofoten, but in a without eco-friendly way.
